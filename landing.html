Rules & Regulations

  • Time Limit: Each question will have a 30-second time limit.
  • Scoring: Correct answers score 10 points, and incorrect answers score 0 points.
  • No Negative Scoring: No negative points for wrong answers.
  • Fair Play: Cheating will lead to disqualification.
  • Leaderboard: The player with the highest score wins!
